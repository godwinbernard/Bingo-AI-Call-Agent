FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot

Our voices are powered by ElevenLabs voice cloning with natural prosody, breathing, and filler words. In blind tests, contacts correctly identify the caller as AI less than 40% of the time. That said, Bingo is always honest if directly asked — we never deny being an AI, which is both ethical and TCPA-compliant.

Bingo uses Claude AI as its reasoning engine, so it handles unexpected questions naturally using the context you provided about your product or service. You can also define fallback behaviors — like transferring to a human agent, offering a callback, or gracefully ending the call — for situations that fall outside the script's scope.

Every number is checked against your uploaded DNC list before dialing — this check runs at the moment of the call, not at upload time. Bingo also enforces configurable calling hours per timezone, adds required disclosure statements, and logs consent signals. We recommend consulting a compliance attorney for your specific use case, but our platform gives you all the technical controls you need.

Growth and Enterprise plans include native integrations with HubSpot and Salesforce — calls, transcripts, and outcomes sync automatically. We also provide full webhook support and a REST API, which means you can connect Bingo to virtually any tool via Zapier, Make, or custom code. Notion, Airtable, and Pipedrive are popular through Zapier.

Starter plans support up to 5 concurrent calls. Growth plans go up to 50. Enterprise plans are uncapped and can be configured for thousands of simultaneous calls with dedicated infrastructure. We've processed over 10,000 concurrent calls for large enterprise customers during peak campaigns.
